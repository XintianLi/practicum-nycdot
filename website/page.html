NYC BIKE

Data Information
The data of bike trips are from NYC DOT, which are GIS tracks. We aggregared half of August data to be visualized here.
The bike stations and bike lanes are from NYC LION.
Instructions
The website aims to help people visualize the biketrips in New York City.
By clicking the buttons under "Let's Visualize" part, we are able to see the visualizations of bike trips, bikelanes, and bike stations.
By choosing the scenarios under the "Scenario" part, we are able to visualize how different conditions influence the number of bike trips.

Let's start finding out the changes of biketrips by choosing different scenarios!
